Finished reading "The Go programming language" book

2022-10-10

After almost 4 years of programming in Go I finally read a book about the language. Go PL is just amazing because of its simplicity. Having even a little experience in programming one could start writing pretty decent code. But writing simple and readable code is always a challenge. What I like about this book is examples: concise & simple.

Definitely worth reading it. If you're experienced Go coder you may only read chapters about Go routines, reflection and unsafe package.
